Take Away

The most thing I’ve learned from the project is that the design method especially the hierarchy structure can be varied for different scenarios. There’s no ONE solution to solve the problem. We should always to think deeper and further. The best way is to fit current users instead of predictive the future. Also, we should keep learning and testing the solution is effective or not to or need modifications.
